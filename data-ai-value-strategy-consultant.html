On my title

I work at Accenture, in the AI & Data Strategy group, out of their Los Angeles office.

I help bring AI solutions into production. Although my formal title on recent projects has been “Quality Engineering Lead,” I typically operate in a broader capacity, owning the client relationship, leading deployment readiness, and making the technical go/no-go recommendation on whether an AI agent is ready.

People sometimes describe me as "working in consulting" — which isn't quite right. Accenture does a lot of things, and while I'd prefer not to be boxed into that label (not because consulting is inherently bad, I just like the idea of owning responsibility), I genuinely enjoy working here. I'm surrounded by talented people and get to work on exciting projects for clients doing meaningful things.
