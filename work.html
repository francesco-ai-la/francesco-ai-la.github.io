Work

I am currently employed at Accenture, in the Data & AI Strategy group, for their Los Angeles office.

Whenever people describe me as "working in consulting" they are wrong. Accenture does a lot of things, and as much as I want to dislike the association of my profession to consulting (not because there is something inherently evil in consulting, I just enjoy the notion of taking on responsibility); I sincerely enjoy working for them, since I am sometimes surrounded by very talented people, and I get to work on exciting projects for really cool and impactful clients.

I work in deploying AI solutions. My official title in my last two projects is "quality engineering lead", but having no engineering background and respecting immensely real engineers, I am by no means an engineer, yet someone that really enjoys working with them. Usually, real synergies are created by my collaboration with them.

While deploying new technologies, such in the case of AI in the current era, there are a lot of novel issues. I find them, I try to solve them, and I work in communicating to clients and internal teams what is happening while pushing things forward.
