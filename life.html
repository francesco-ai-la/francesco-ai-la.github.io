Life

I am still trying to understand the ideal interconnectedness between work and life. I accept and reject all the theories I have encountered so far:

  • the Silicon Valley dismissal of conventional work-life balance in favor of total commitment to building your business
  • the Jeff Bezos work-life harmony
  • the other various hybrid options

So far I embrace the traditional view of my Italian culture: work and life should be clearly separated. Maybe that's why I haven't "made it big" (whatever that means) in my professional California-based tech career.
